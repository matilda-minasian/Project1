Traditional Music

  • Yarkhushta
    Yarkhushta is a traditional Armenian warrior dance song. It is performed during cultural celebrations and represents strength, unity, and the historical spirit of Armenian fighters.

Modern Armenian Music

  • Kef Chlini
    Kef Chlini by Ladaniva is a modern Armenian song that blends traditional Armenian folk influences with contemporary musical styles.

National and Iconic Songs

  • Mer Hayreniq
    Mer Hayreniq is the national anthem of Armenia and one of the most important patriotic songs representing Armenian independence and identity.
  • La Bohème (Charles Aznavour)
    La Bohème is one of the most famous songs by the Armenian-French singer Charles Aznavour, one of the most internationally recognized Armenian artists.

Popular Armenian Dance Music

  • Parumenk Menk Haykakan
    Parumenk Menk Haykakan is a popular Armenian dance anthem often played at weddings, celebrations, and cultural festivals.
